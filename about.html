Welcome to B·KidZanian

Ever wonder what being KidZanian is like? As a CitiZen of KidZania, your child will unlock more activities, earn more kidZos and have more fun!

Joining is easy.

We'll ask for a few details just like you would provide for a real PaZZport. When you visit KidZania, go to the PaZZport Office to take your child's photo and receive an official KidZania PaZZport.

Get ready to have more fun!

The more skills that CitiZens learn to build a better world, the more benefits they'll earn. Naturalized CitiZens receive a stamp in their PaZZport and an additional 2 kidZos for each activity they complete, plus 5% off at the National Store.

Advance to Distinguished CitiZenship with 30 stamps in the PaZZport and enjoy express access to KidZania, special activities reserved just for Distinguished CitiZens, a free beverage at the Parent's Lounge, 10% off at the National Store, and access to special offers and events. Upon achieving 60 total stamps, Honorable CitiZens enjoy all the benefits of being KidZanian, as well as eligibility for the KidZania CongreZZ, as leaders to design the next generation experience at KidZania.

Get ready to build a better world.

B·KidZanian is available to children ages 16 years and under with their parent or legal guardian's consent. A nominal fee is charged upon delivery of the PaZZport.
