FAQs

Here are all the basics you need to know about B•KidZanian, with top questions grouped by category. Just click on any question to see more information in that category.

Sign up

How can I become a Naturalized CitiZen?

Joining B•KidZanian is easy and it’s free. The next time you visit KidZania, please visit the KidZania PaZZport Office. You can also sign up online at www.BKidZanian.com with your parent’s assistance.

Who is eligible to be a member of B•KidZanian?

B•KidZanian is open to children ages 17 years and under.

What do I need to do to get a PaZZport?

When you visit the KidZania PaZZport Office, we will ask you a few questions to establish your identity, take your picture, and give you your PaZZport to use that very same day. It is important to have your parent’s authorization to participate in the full program. Parents can provide authorization at the PaZZport Office or online at www.BKidZanian.com.

Sign In

How do I sign in online?

Viewing your B•KidZanian activity is easy online. When you visit www.BKidZanian.com, you’ll be asked to provide your PaZZport number, or email address, or User ID, plus a password. Parents may log in with their email address or User ID, plus a password.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Need to reset your password? No problem. Please click on the Forgot Password link at www.BKidZanian.com and enter the email address associated with the account. CitiZens who have an online profile may be prompted to enter a parent’s email address if he/she does not have an email address with B•KidZanian.

PaZZport management

I have a PaZZport.

How do I link it to an online account?

Linking your PaZZport to a new online account is easy. You can enter your PaZZport number at www.BKidZanian.com. We will ask you to verify your information and then create a memorable User Name and password.

How do I get a Distinguished or Honorable PaZZport?

B•KidZanian provides three levels of recognition: CitiZen, Distinguished CitiZen, and Honorable Citizen. When you visit KidZania, please bring your PaZZport. For every activity that you complete, we will put a special stamp in your PaZZport. When you have collected 30 stamps, please visit the KidZania PaZZport Office to receive your Distinguished PaZZport. Distinguished CitiZens who collect an additional 30 stamps will be recognized as Honorable Citizens. We are pleased to offer special activities and benefits for our Distinguished and Honorable CitiZens. To learn more, please visit levels.

I forgot to bring my PaZZport to the park. What do I do?

You can still enjoy the benefits of B•KidZanian if you forgot your PaZZport. Please visit the KidZania PaZZport Office, where we will provide you with a temporary PaZZport. Collect your stamps at each activity and we will automatically add them to your collection. I lost my PaZZport and would like to get another one. How do I do that? In case you have lost your PaZZport, we can assist you and issue a new PaZZport. Please visit the KidZania PaZZport Office. We will ask you a few questions to establish your identity, take your picture, and reissue the PaZZport. Please note that you may be required to pay a nominal fee in kidZos to replace your PaZZport.

Change info

How do I change my User ID or password?

If you have already created an online profile, you may update your profile any time. Please log in to your account at www.BKidZanian.com and click on the link to Edit CitiZen Profile or Edit Parent Profile. You may select a unique User ID or change your password. You may also change your password by clicking on the Forgot Password link and entering the email address associated with your online account.

How do I change my personal information online?

If you have already created an online profile, you may update your profile any time. Please log in to your account at www.BKidZanian.com and click on the link to Edit CitiZen Profile or Edit Parent Profile.

How do I change my communication preferences?

If you have already created an online profile, you may update your communication preferences any time. Please log into your account at www.BKidZanian.com and click on the link to Edit Parent Profile. Certain service messaging is required in order for you to manage your account, and you may choose to receive or discontinue promotional messages from KidZania.

Delete account

I would like to discontinue my participation in B•KidZanian. How do I do that?

We hope that you will stay with us at KidZania, but understand that you may choose to discontinue your participation. Closing an account is easy – just log in to your account at www.BKidZanian.com and click on Edit Parent Profile. Choose the Close Account link where you will be asked to confirm your selection. Please note that if you close the parent account, all CitiZen accounts associated with that profile will be closed as well.

KidZania partners

What is a KidZania Operator?

KidZania is a global company with a presence in several countries. Franchised Operators are not owned by KidZania, and are integral to delivering the KidZania experience around the world.

What is a KidZania Affiliate?

An affiliate is another company that is wholly owned or controlled by a KidZania company. It may take several KidZania companies working together to provide you with programs and services.

What is a KidZania Service Provider?

KidZania works with specially selected third party service providers to facilitate delivery of the B•KidZanian program. This includes, but is not limited to, the delivery of communications and the production of all materials required to support the program. The information that you share with KidZania may be shared with KidZania service providers only to the extent that it is required for delivery of the B•KidZanian program.
